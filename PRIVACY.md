## Privacy Policy for TabStack

Last updated: 2026-02-26

TabStack is a Chrome extension that copies selected tab information as Markdown-formatted text to your clipboard.

### Data Access

TabStack accesses only the titles and URLs of your currently selected browser tabs. This information is used solely to generate Markdown-formatted text for the clipboard copy operation.

### Data Collection and Storage

TabStack does not collect, store, or retain any data. Tab titles and URLs are processed in memory only for the duration of the copy operation and are not saved anywhere.

### Data Transmission

TabStack does not send any data to external servers. There are no analytics, telemetry, or network requests of any kind. All processing happens entirely on your local machine.

### Third-Party Sharing

TabStack does not share any data with third parties.

### Cookies and Tracking

TabStack does not use cookies, local storage, or any tracking mechanisms.
